    GENERAL OVERVIEW

    🧠

    Our Data

    Our analysis utilized a publicly accessible Kaggle dataset to examine academic paper retractions. We processed and cleaned this data to categorize retractions by country and visualized the findings through an interactive plot.

    🎢

    Our Graphs

    We implemented several visualization methods to represent the retraction data, featuring a choropleth map with heat density to identify geographic concentrations of retractions. The interface enables users to select individual countries and explore their data through complementary Sankey and pie chart visualizations.

    🌐

    Visualisation

    We developed an interactive choropleth map using Plotly that allows users to click on any country to explore its specific retraction data. The interface provides flexible viewing options through a dropdown menu, enabling toggling between Sankey flow diagrams and categorical pie charts for comprehensive data examination.
